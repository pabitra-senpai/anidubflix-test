Privacy Regulations

We value your privacy above all. Anidubflix operates on direct client-side mechanism structures, meaning we use local technologies (such as LocalStorage inside your browser) to track features like "Continue Watching" progress and "Bookmarked Items".

None of this personal progress status or browsing tracking leaves your web browser context or is sent to any external server databases. Your streaming and watch lists remain entirely in your control.
